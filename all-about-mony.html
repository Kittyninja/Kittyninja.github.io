ALL ABOUT MONY

Mony is an old cat that lives in the Swanson house. She is frequently attacked and nipped at by the younger Louis, though Monk usually stays peaceful with her. She was a stray until the Swansons took her in in 2006 to become their pet. At 15 years old or so, she is the oldest and the wisest of the Swanson kitties.
