All About Louis

Louis is a kitten that loves to be cute, annoy Monk, and be vicious to his owners. He nips and sometimes attacks the poor old Mony.

Louis's Biggest Habits

  1. Annoy Monk
  2. Go Crazy
  3. Be Playful
  4. Look Cute
  5. Curl Up And Sleep

Louis's Birth Information

Louis is still a kitten at 8 months old. He has not grown to full size yet and was born in 2019. He was taken to his current home by the Swansons. He originally came from a foster home in "Austin Pets Alive!" and was named by the Swansons after Louis Armstrong.

Other Information

Louis is known to steal attention from his older brother, Monk. He freaks out a lot and seems to like to run around and play. He also loves to stalk and hunt things. (Unsucsessfully.)
